# Lighthouse — Analysis Recommendations

## Tier 1 Analyses

### #7 — Cost Per Successful Reintegration
Total funds ÷ completed reintegrations per safehouse.
- **Landing page** hero stat — one big number, one line of context
- **Impact page** — expand with a per-safehouse bar chart

### #5 — Length of Stay vs. Risk Level Reduction
Months stayed vs. drop between `initial_risk_level` and `current_risk_level`.
- Grouped bar chart (bucketed by stay length) for donor audiences
- Scatter plot if you want to show the raw correlation

### #3 — Intervention Plan Completion vs. Health/Education Scores
Completed vs. incomplete plans compared against health and education scores.
- Side-by-side bars or a simple summary card for donors
- Box plot for internal use

---

## Placement

| Page | What goes there |
|---|---|
| Landing | #7 as a single hero stat |
| Impact/Results | #7 → #5 → #3, in that order |
